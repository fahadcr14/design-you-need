### Color Craft Insights
- **[8]** The entire palette is a single hue (teal ~175°) at varying saturation/lightness — extreme discipline that creates cohesion without monotony
- **[8]** The warm cream #F8F6EE vs cool navy #091523 creates maximum contrast without harshness — the parchment undertone unifies all botanical illustrations seamlessly
- **[8]** The palette avoids both clinical blue and stereotypical pink — the mauve-lavender range feels gender-aware without being reductive, and the warm neutral base prevents the coldness typical of health apps
- **[8]** The red anatomical renders serve double duty as both content imagery AND the warm accent color, eliminating need for a separate warm palette entry — very efficient color economy
- **[8]** The entire palette is derived from a single warm-earth hue family — no blue or cool tones anywhere, creating an unusually cohesive and distinctive brand feel in a space dominated by clinical blues
- **[8]** The warm gray (#A1A19D) hero background is unusual for healthtech — avoids sterile white/blue cliché while maintaining clinical credibility through typography and imagery
- **[7]** The entire palette is derived from the nature photography's color space — every UI color could be sampled from the wildflower and savanna images, creating unconscious visual harmony
- **[7]** The card surface color (#1A1A1E) is just 6-8% lighter than bg — senior-level restraint in dark theme elevation
- **[7]** The warm brown-black text color (#130E0B) instead of pure black is a deliberate choice that harmonizes with the orange palette — a senior move that unifies the entire color story
- **[7]** The teal-to-mint spectrum creates a cohesive temperature while gold breaks it surgically at conversion points only

## Design Recipes

### Score 8/10 — minimal-corporate — AI-powered food allergen detection via protein structure ana

**Design Recipe:** Use a monochromatic teal palette: #354B4A for text, #45827D for accents, #A9D3D5 for decorative, #FEFEFE for backgrounds. Set Inter or SF Pro at 68px bold tight-tracked for H1, 44px bold for H2, 17px regular for body. Wrap entire page in a rounded container (border-radius: 20px) with a soft box-shadow to create a device-like frame. Feature cards in a 2-column CSS Grid with 24px gap, white fill, 16px radius, 36px padding, thin #E5EFF0 border. All buttons are pills (border-radius: 999px) — primary dark-filled, secondary outline with arrow icons.

**Dev Recipe:** Next.js + Tailwind CSS with CSS custom properties for the teal HSL scale. Spline or pre-rendered 3D for the hero protein visual, Framer Motion for scroll-triggered card reveals, and a sticky nav with backdrop-filter:blur(12px).

**Core Lesson:** A single-hue monochromatic palette with a bespoke hero visual that literally represents your product's core concept creates more brand impact than any multi-color scheme.

**Steal These:**
- Page-as-device: rounded outer container with diffused shadow creates premium framing
- Monochromatic palette from a single hue at 5+ lightness stops
- Hero visual that IS the product concept (protein shapes = protein analysis)
